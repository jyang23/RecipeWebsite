General Information and Inquiries

Here you'll find information on our convenient campuses and centers, directories for people and for buildings, feedback forms, and some important contacts for campus-wide departments and programs.

For general questions or information about Montgomery College, please email response@montgomerycollege.edu or call us at 240-567-5000. Please note: the Response Center answers emails and phone calls from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Monday through Friday.

For general questions or information about our noncredit Workforce Development & Continuing Education programs, please email wdce@montgomerycollege.edu or call 240-567-5188. In general, WD&CE Customer Service hours are 8:30 a.m. to 8:30 p.m. Monday through Friday, with Saturday hours from 8:30 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
